ux-qa loop-back rule — mechanical proof
=======================================

Claim under test (garrison-ux-qa, 'Severity threshold + loop-back (A8)'):
  a finding AT OR ABOVE policy.uxQa.severityThreshold is BLOCKING and loops the
  slice back to garrison-implement; a finding BELOW it is a note and advances.

Mechanism: the DoD checker that the Kanban Validate list runs, which parses the
verdict from its LAST stdout line (Done | Implement).
  validator: fittings/seed/garrison-skills/.apm/skills/garrison-validate/scripts/validate.mjs
  node:      v20.19.4
  run at:    2026-07-11T05:06:52Z

Method: two synthetic kind=ui slices, IDENTICAL in every gate (tests/typecheck/lint/
build/e2e exit 0; adversarialReview approve; adversarialTest pass; video verified),
differing ONLY in the severity of the uxQa findings. severityThreshold = major in both.

RESULT: PROVEN. The single variable is the finding severity:
  major (>= threshold) -> 'VALIDATE check uxQa: FAIL - 1 finding(s) >= major' -> Implement

reaches Done. Blocking is decided by severity vs threshold, exactly as specified.
